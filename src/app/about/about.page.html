About About Application AgriPrice

Get agricultural commodity prices within Trinidad and Tobago. Rather than burden users with constantly checking for price changes, the AgriPrice application provides an interactive way for users to view and get notified of the ever-changing crop prices within Trinidad and Tobago. With the help of interactive graphs, users can also view the change in prices over an extended period of time.

Developed by the AgriNeTT Team associated with the University of the West Indies(St.Augustine).
